info About Grove Guardian

Grove Guardian is your community tree care companion, designed to help coordinate and track the watering of young trees in our neighborhood. The app makes it easy to identify which trees need water and maintain a healthy urban forest.

Why Tree Care Matters

Young trees need regular watering for the first two years after planting. Your participation helps ensure their survival and contributes to a greener, healthier community.

How to Water Trees
